Growing Microgreens

Growing microgreens is a fair simple process. A start-up kit includes a growing tray, some coconut fiber growing medium, seeds, an LED light, and a spray bottle to help keep your plants healthy and hydrated. And that’s it! No soil, no fertilizers, no chemicals. This is the “grow pure method” that City-Hydro prides itself on.

To begin growing, it is as easy as 1, 2, 3. You simply place your grow medium in the grow tray, cover liberally with seeds so you have one packed layer, then spray the seeds down with water from your spray bottle (this helps prevent the seeds from moving around too much, too). Soon enough the seeds will start to sprout! In about seven days, you will see the first two leaves appear on the plant, (these are called the cotyledon leaves) which means you are ready to harvest and enjoy your microgreens.
